The AISA Launchpad 1.0.0 is an open sourced javafx implementation 
to provide the list of the world space agencies with their logos.
Every space agency in the world is identified with four letters.
Here the list of the Space Agencies : 
AISA : The Arabo-Islamic Space Agency shortly the AISA Space Agency
NASA : The National Aeronautics Space Administration of the United States of America
ESSA : The European Space Agency with its 23 member states
CCCP : The Soviet and Russian Space Agency
INSA : The National Space and Research Organization of India
ZHGO : The National Space Administration of China
NZSA : The New Zealand Space Agency
Every space agency with its logo is created as a button and you can click on the button 
to access the desired space-agency-website without losing time to type the url.
The logos are shown transparently and styled with CSS.
